Django migrations without downtimes

Jul 14, 2015 · 12 minute read Most web applications use a relational database. The database schema needs to be changed over time as features are added, changed or removed. These database migrations need to be designed and integrated in your deployment process. On the one hand, scheduled downtimes can be expensive for the business and are painful to organize for larger sites or organizations. On the other hand, applying migrations on a live system can bring down your web-server in counter-intuitive ways. Read On →
